"The Greatest Gift: Finding Hope and Salvation"

God loves every single soul and desires that no one should perish. Our mission is to share the message of salvation with those who have not yet heard or accepted this gift of eternal life through Jesus Christ.

A - Admit you are a sinner. The Bible says, "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God" (Romans 3:23). Recognize that no one is perfect and that our sins carry a penalty, which is spiritual death (Romans 6:23).

B - Believe in Jesus Christ. The Bible assures us that God demonstrates His love for us through the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ (Romans 5:8). "If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved" (Romans 10:9).

C - Confess Jesus as your Lord and Savior. "For it is with your heart that you believe and are justified, and it is with your mouth that you confess and are saved" (Romans 10:10). Confess your faith and let others know about your decision to follow Christ.
