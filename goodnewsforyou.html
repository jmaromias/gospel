The Gift of Salvation

For God so loved the world, that He gave His only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in Him should not perish, but have everlasting life. - John 3:16

Salvation is a free gift from God, available to all who believe in Jesus Christ. Here is a simple way to understand how to accept this gift:

A - Admit that you are a sinner. (Romans 3:23, Romans 6:23)

B - Believe that Jesus Christ died for your sins and rose again. (Romans 5:8, Romans 10:9)

C - Confess Jesus as your Lord and Savior. (Romans 10:10, 1 John 1:9)
